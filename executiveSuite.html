Room Description

The executive rooms are specifically made smaller than our family rooms for business men or women looking for a luxurious hotel to stay in. It fully offers the luxury and comfort our family room or other rooms provide.

  • Bedroom: 24.17 m2
  • Bathroom: 7.64 m2

Room Accessories

The room comes with a single bedroom with air conditioning, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a fridge, a shower, and a wardrobe. A continental breakfast is available each morning , there are equipped with a 40-inch Smart TV, USB hubs as well as a wet-room style bathroom with a shower, heated floors and towel rails. The room features a kitchenette, Tea-Coffee Making Facilities, and a Mini-Bar and a fully functional Dstv dish.
